Old Fashioned

Description

The name calls for it: This amazing drink has been a must-have for thousands of bars for over 200 years.
The classic definition of cocktail called for 4 ingredients: spirits, sugar, water and bitters. The Old Fashioned has it all.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 oz Maker's Mark
  • 3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 tbsp water
    • Orange twist for garnishing

Steps

  1. Add the sugar and bitters into a mixing glass, then add the water, and stir until the sugar is nearly dissolved.
  2. Fill the mixing glass with ice, add the bourbon, and stir until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass over one large ice cube.
  4. Add ice to your shaker and give it another shake
  5. Express the oil of an orange twist over the glass, then drop into the glass to garnish.
  6. Enjoy!
