Whiskey Sour

Description

This classic recipe yields only for a few ingredients, but brings a complex and amazing taste.
Whether you are a bourbon lover or not, a well made whiskey sour is always great to sip in a classy bar or at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Woodford Reserve bourbon
  • 0.75 oz lemon juice
  • 0.75 oz simple syrup
  • 1 egg white
    • Drops of Angosture bitters

Steps

  1. Prepare a shaker, your ingredients, and ice
  2. Add the bourbon, simple syrup, lemon juice and the egg white to your shaker
  3. Give it a firm dry shake for about 30 minutes
  4. Add ice to your shaker and give it another shake
  5. Pour the mix into a rocks glass through a strainger, then add a few drops of Abgostura bitter on the foam
  6. Enjoy!
