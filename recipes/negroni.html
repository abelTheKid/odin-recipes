Negroni

Description

This smooth yet vigorous cocktail is one of the most basic, most ancient ones ever existed.
With three simple ingredients it seems like an easy way to make a classic, yet it can be a challenging task to make it right.

Ingredients

  • Gin 1prt
  • Sweet vermouth 1prt
  • Campari 1prt
  • Orange peel for garnishing

Steps

  1. Prepare a mixing glass, your ingredients, and ice
  2. Add the gin, sweet vermouth and Campari to your mixing glass
  3. Carefully place a large block of ice in the mixture
  4. Give it a strong whirl with your barspoon
  5. Pour the mix into a rocks glass through a strainger, then add a block of ice to it
  6. Peel a slice off an orange, press the oil around the edge of the glass, then add the peel to the drink
  7. Enjoy!
